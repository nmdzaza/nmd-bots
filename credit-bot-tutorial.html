10
Mail Your Letters & Wait for Results
Bureaus have 30 days to respond by law

Once your letters are sent, the credit bureaus have 30 days under the FCRA to investigate each dispute. If they can't verify a negative item, they are required by law to remove it.

Day 1 — Letters mailed
You send certified mail to Equifax, TransUnion, and Experian (and any other targets). Return receipt requested so you get proof of delivery.
Days 3–5 — Bureaus receive your letters
Certified mail typically arrives within 3–5 business days. The 30-day clock starts the day they receive it.
Days 30–45 — Bureau responses arrive
Bureaus mail back their investigation results. Items they couldn't verify will be listed as removed or updated.
Round 2 (if needed)
Anything that survived Round 1 gets a fresh strategy. Come back to the bot, start a new order, and we target what's left with escalated letters.
📈
Most clients see score movement within 30–60 days of mailing. Results depend on your specific items — some disputes remove accounts immediately, others take 2–3 rounds. Keep your receipts and bureau responses. Come back for Round 2 if needed — same price, new letters.

Got Questions?
Client FAQ
The most common questions from first-time clients.
💰 How much does it cost?
The Big 3 bureaus (Equifax, TransUnion, Experian) are completely free — letters generated and delivered at no charge. Paid services like banking blacklists (ChexSystems, EWS, Clarity), data brokers (LexisNexis, CoreLogic, etc.), or debt collectors are $30 each. One-time payment, no subscription, no monthly fees.
⚡ How long until I get my letters?
Minutes — not days. The moment your payment is confirmed (or instantly for free orders), the AI analyzes your report and delivers your custom PDF directly in the Telegram chat. Most clients have their letters in 2–5 minutes.
₿ I've never used Bitcoin. What do I do?
Tap the "Don't have Bitcoin? Buy it here →" button in the payment screen — it takes you to a simple card checkout where you pay by Visa or Mastercard and Bitcoin is sent automatically. Or tap Support and the team will walk you through it personally.
📋 Where do I get my credit report?
The official free source is annualcreditreport.com — that's the government-authorized site. You can pull all three bureaus for free. Screenshot or save the PDF, then send it to the bot. Credit Karma, Experian's app, or any bureau's website also works.
⚖️ Is this legal?
100%. The Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) gives every American the legal right to dispute inaccurate, outdated, or unverifiable items on their credit report. Bureaus are required by law to investigate within 30 days. We just help you do it with the right letters.
📈 How long until my score goes up?
Your letters arrive in minutes. Once you mail them, bureaus have up to 30 days to respond under the FCRA. If they can't verify a negative item, they must remove it. Most clients start seeing score movement within 30–60 days of sending their letters. Round 2 targets anything that survived Round 1.
